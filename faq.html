Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

Beats of Beirut is a premier event advertising platform dedicated to promoting the most exciting concerts, festivals, and entertainment experiences in Lebanon.

You can subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or regularly check our website for the latest event announcements.

Tickets and Reservations

Tickets can be purchased through our official website or through authorized ticketing partners listed on our event pages.

Refund policies vary by event. Please refer to the specific event page for detailed information on ticket refunds.

Contact and Support

Contact information for event organizers is provided on each event page. Alternatively, you can reach out to us through our contact page.

For media inquiries, please email us at media@beatsofbeirut.com.
